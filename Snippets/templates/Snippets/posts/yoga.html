Lifestyle Tips

Yoga , as we all know has the power to change a person's lifestyle . You may not believe me but that's okay because I am here to make you believe it . You may think how do I know that ? Well answer to that , I am a yoga practitioner since 2014 and yoga has not only given me flexibility physically but has also made me mentally strong and has taught me to how to think rationally in difficult situations .


Meditation is one powerful tool of yoga which makes you mentally strong . Along with meditation , regular practice of power yoga can give you physical strength as well .


Yoga is important however during the hectic day of an individual , it's hard to find a time space to practice your own sequences . This is where the 5-10-20 rule I came up with comes to place .


" The 5-10-20 Rule : Make about 5 unique sequences that includes 2-5 of your 10 yoga poses and do yoga for 20 minutes which also includes sparing 5 minutes to your breathing exercise ."

It's obvious that you will be bored of performing the same sequences almost everyday so I suggest let's have 5 different sequences and repeat it periodically . So now you know what the 5 in rule means . The next step is to list down the 10 major challenging yoga poses you enjoy .


Now is the final step , your goal for every day is to perform a sequence within 20 minutes and this sequence can includes about 2-5 of your ten yoga poses you love to do . Apart from these yoga poses , spare about 5 minutes on your breathing .


Let's end this blog . Hope to see your interest continue in all my other blogs.
